About St. Stephen

Our faith community and mission

Our Mission

St. Stephen is a vibrant and welcoming faith community, blessed with multicultural diversity, and united with love for the Eucharist.

Sharing in the mission of Jesus Christ to "make disciples of all nations," we commit ourselves to the evangelization of all our parishioners with charity, courage, and humble service.

Our Tagline

Faith, Love and Truth

Church History

St. Stephen Catholic Church has served the Chattanooga community for generations. Our beautiful brick church building, with its distinctive arched entryways, stands as a symbol of our enduring faith and commitment to serving God and our neighbors.

We welcome people of all backgrounds and invite all who seek a loving, faith-filled community.

Leadership

Pastor

Spiritual leader and shepherd of our parish community. Available for counseling, guidance, and pastoral care.
