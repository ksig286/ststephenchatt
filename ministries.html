Get Involved

At St. Stephen, everyone has a role to play. We invite you to share your time, talent, and treasure through our many ministries and service opportunities.

Prayer Ministry

Join us in intercessory prayer. Participate in our prayer groups, rosary devotions, and adoration hours.

Liturgical Ministries

Serve as a lector, Eucharistic minister, altar server, or in music ministry. Help lead our community in worship.

Fellowship Ministry

Build community through gatherings, game days, celebrations, and social events that unite our parish family.

Pastoral Care Ministry

Bring comfort and support to those who are sick, homebound, or grieving. Visit, pray with, and encourage our members.

Social Action Ministry

Live out our Catholic faith by serving those in need through community outreach and social justice efforts.

Health Ministry

Support the physical and spiritual health of our parish members through care, resources, and compassionate service.

Ministry with Children & Youth

Nurture the faith of our young people through Christian formation, youth programs, and mentorship.

New Evangelization Ministry

Help share the good news of Christ with those seeking faith. Invite, welcome, and accompany newcomers on their spiritual journey.

Adult Faith Formation

Deepen your knowledge of the faith through adult Bible study, FORMED, and other learning opportunities.

Ready to Serve?
