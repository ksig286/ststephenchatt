How Your Gift is Used

Every gift to St. Stephen supports:

  • ✓ Worship & Liturgy
    Maintaining our beautiful church and providing meaningful celebrations
  • ✓ Faith Formation
    Programs for children, youth, and adults to grow in their faith
  • ✓ Pastoral Care
    Supporting the sick, homebound, and those in spiritual need
  • ✓ Community Outreach
    Serving the poor, hungry, and marginalized in our community
  • ✓ Administration
    Staff, utilities, and operational support to keep our parish running

Parish Budget & Transparency

We are committed to transparent stewardship of your gifts. Our parish budget is available for review. If you have questions about how funds are used, please contact our office.

Tax-Deductible Giving

St. Stephen Catholic Church is a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization. All charitable gifts are tax-deductible. We provide contribution statements upon request.
